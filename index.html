How it works

Enter household balances, contributions, retirement assumptions, pension and Social Security details, plus optional child college and wedding support. Retirement age means both: contributions stop and retirement spending starts. Withdrawals follow: Brokerage → IRA → 401(k), and 401(k) is only available from age 60 in this model.

Retirement & Planning

Retirement Age Optimization

Earliest feasible: first retirement age or pair with no shortfall and no depletion.

Maximum end assets: among successful results only, choose the retirement age or pair with the most assets remaining at planning end age.

Safety margin: earliest successful retirement age or pair with ending assets above your safety target.

If no successful result exists, fallback is: do not retire in the model.

Withdrawal Strategy

Default withdrawal order: Brokerage → IRA → 401(k)

Retirement starts at retirement age, and contributions stop at retirement age.

401(k) withdrawals are only allowed from the penalty-free age assumption entered above.
